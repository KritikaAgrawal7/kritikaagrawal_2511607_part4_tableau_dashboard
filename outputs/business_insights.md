# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

An executive sales dashboard was developed in Tableau to analyze business performance across sales, profitability, customer segments, regions, categories, shipping performance, discounts, and product returns for the period 2024–2025.

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators, identify high-performing areas, and recognize opportunities for operational improvements.

---

## Key Performance Indicators

The dashboard includes the following KPI cards:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin

These KPIs provide an overall summary of business performance.

---

## Key Business Insights

### 1. Sales Trend

- Sales remained relatively stable throughout 2024–2025 with periodic fluctuations.
- No significant long-term decline was observed.
- Seasonal peaks and dips can be further investigated for campaign planning.

---

### 2. Regional Performance

- Certain states generated substantially higher sales and profit than others.
- Regional comparison helps identify high-performing markets for future expansion.
- Lower-performing regions may require targeted marketing or operational improvements.

---

### 3. Category Profitability

- Technology generated the highest overall profits.
- Office Supplies contributed moderate profits.
- Furniture generated comparatively lower profitability.

This suggests that product mix plays an important role in overall profitability.

---

### 4. Customer Segment Performance

- Customer segments contributed differently to overall sales.
- Home Office and Consumer segments generated strong revenue.
- Segment-wise analysis helps prioritize customer acquisition and retention strategies.

---

### 5. Shipping Performance

- Standard Class accounted for the majority of orders.
- Shipping delay buckets help identify delivery efficiency across shipping methods.
- Monitoring delayed deliveries can improve customer satisfaction.

---

### 6. Discount vs Profit

- Higher discount percentages generally resulted in lower profits.
- Excessive discounting may reduce business profitability.
- Balanced pricing strategies are recommended.

---

### 7. Return Analysis

- Product returns vary across categories.
- Return Rate provides additional context beyond total returned orders.
- Categories with higher return rates should be investigated for quality or customer satisfaction issues.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Continue focusing on profitable product categories.
- Review discount policies to avoid unnecessary profit erosion.
- Improve delivery performance for delayed shipments.
- Monitor return-heavy categories to reduce operational losses.
- Increase investments in high-performing regions and customer segments.
