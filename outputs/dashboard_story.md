# Dashboard Story

## Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to provide executives with a single view of overall business performance across sales, profitability, customer behavior, operations, and returns.

The dashboard supports strategic decision making by highlighting key trends and performance indicators.

---

# Story Flow

## Executive Overview

The dashboard begins with three KPI cards:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin

These provide an immediate snapshot of business performance.

---

## Sales Performance

The Sales Trend View illustrates how sales changed over time.

Executives can quickly identify periods of growth, decline, and seasonal variation.

---

## Regional Analysis

The Regional Performance View compares sales across states while simultaneously showing profitability.

This helps identify:

- Best-performing markets
- Underperforming regions
- Opportunities for regional expansion

---

## Product Performance

The Category Profitability View analyzes profits across product categories and sub-categories.

This enables product managers to understand which product groups generate the highest business value.

---

## Customer Analysis

The Customer Segment View compares sales performance across customer segments.

Understanding segment performance helps improve marketing strategies and customer targeting.

---

## Operational Performance

The Shipping Performance View evaluates shipping methods and delivery delays.

This allows business leaders to monitor operational efficiency and customer service performance.

---

## Pricing Strategy

The Discount vs Profit View examines the relationship between discount percentage and profit.

This visualization supports pricing decisions by highlighting the financial impact of discounting.

---

## Returns Analysis

The Return Analysis View identifies categories with higher returned orders and return rates.

This helps uncover quality issues, customer dissatisfaction, or operational inefficiencies.

---

# Dashboard Interactivity

The dashboard includes interactive filters that allow users to analyze performance by:

- Region
- Customer Segment
- Ship Mode
- Category

Interactive filtering enables executives to drill down into specific business areas.

---

# Business Value

This executive dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor organizational performance
- Identify profitable regions and categories
- Optimize pricing strategies
- Improve shipping operations
- Reduce product returns
- Make data-driven business decisions

## Dashboard Interactivity

The dashboard includes interactive filtering to support exploratory analysis.

- Selecting a state in the **Regional Sales & Profit Performance** chart filters all other dashboard views.
- KPI cards, sales trends, customer segment performance, shipping performance, discount analysis, and return analysis update dynamically based on the selected state.
- Users can clear the selection to return to the overall business view.

This interaction enables executives to quickly analyze the performance of individual regions without navigating to separate reports.
